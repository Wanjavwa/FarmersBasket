About the Event

FarmersBasket is a local initiative that brings farmers and consumers together.

On here farmers can sell their produce directly to the consumer at favorable prices without major retail interferance.

This helps to empower famers, lower the cost of produce for consumers and helps to reduce food waste. FarmersBasket welcomes you!
